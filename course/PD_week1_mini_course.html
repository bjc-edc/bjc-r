Welcome to the Beauty and Joy of Computing, BJC Professional Development

This is an excerpt from the full BJC course, for use only during a professional development workshop.

For six-week workshops: You will use this mini-course only in the first face-to-face week. In the following weeks, you will study these units again, in their complete form.

For three-week workshops: You will use this mini-course throughout the workshop. You will study the complete units during the school year, on your own and in webinars.

You are expected to come out of this experience with an understanding of the breadth of the course, not of each unit in depth.

Unit 1: Introduction to Snap! Programming
Unit 2: Conditionals and Abstraction
Unit 3: Lists
Unit 4: The Internet and Social Impact (just the Data parts)
Unit 5: Algorithms and Data
Unit 6: Trees and Other Fractals

Welcome to the Beauty and Joy of Computing

The Beauty and Joy of Computing for New York City project is adapting UC Berkeley's introductory computer science course as a high school AP CS Principles course. Beauty and Joy of Computing (BJC) uses the snap visual programming language, includes deep CS ideas (functional programming, recursion, and higher-order functions), and addresses the social implications of computing. BJC4NYC will be introduced to 100 NYC high schools over 2015-18.

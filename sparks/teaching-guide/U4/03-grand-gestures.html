Lab 3: Supervised ML with Grand Gestures  

(from CITE AI Proposal)
a. Participants will write a program that takes user-drawn line art and represents it as a list of numbers that can be used to recognize the shape
b. The program will animate the drawing depending on its shape (e.g., a star will blink, a heart will beat, etc.)
c. Participants will then learn a bit about neural networks and visualizations of how the neural network learns.
d. Finally, they will see how neural networks can be used to re-create the project they made.

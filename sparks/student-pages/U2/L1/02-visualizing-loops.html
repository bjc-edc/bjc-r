Looping Your Song

    Repeating the same set of commands (such as with repeat or forever) is called looping or iteration.

  1. If you haven't already, try using the repeat block to repeat a note or a sequence of notes in a song. Play your song with Visible Stepping turned on.
  2. Try nesting one repeat inside another as in the "Für Elise" example above.
  3. Try using a forever block to repeat a command (or group of commands) forever, and play your song with Visible Stepping on.
    The sequence of blocks inside the forever block will repeat until you stop it. You can click the red stop sign () to stop all scripts in your project from running, or you can click an individual script to stop it.
In this activity, you used iteration by repeating a set of commands.

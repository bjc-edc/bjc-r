Revisit Your Plan

  1. Revisit your plan for the costumes and backgrounds for your interactive project.
    • Will they ever change costumes?
      • You can create some kinds of animation effects just by changing costumes. In both of these examples, the sprite isn't moving; it's just changing it's costume.
    • Will the sprites move? If so, how will they move (instantly vs. slowly, change direction)?
    • Will they say or think anything?

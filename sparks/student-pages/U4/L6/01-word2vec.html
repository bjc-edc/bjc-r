Introduction to Embeddings

In this activity, you'll get a quick introduction to the Word2Vec model for making computers understand human languages.

Consider using https://www.cs.cmu.edu/~dst/WordEmbeddingDemo/index.html and review https://ojs.aaai.org/index.php/AAAI/article/view/35197/37352 --MF, 6/2/26

Natural language processing is a type of AI that allows computers to understand and generate human language. Word2Vec is a form of natural language processing that treats each word as vector, a coordinate in space that's often drawn as an arrow.

Computers don't naturally understand language the way you do, but they are built to compute (do math), so you can use mathematical systems like coordinates to teach a computer language. For example, if the computer knows the coordinates of three words like "dog," "puppy," and "cat," it can use math to locate the word "kitten."

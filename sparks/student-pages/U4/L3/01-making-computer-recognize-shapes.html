Gestures

In this activity, you will consider the importance of gestures in life and in computing.

Gestures are movements or positions of the body or head (especially hands, arms, and face) that express an idea, emotion, attitude, intention, etc. Recognizing gestures is an important skill in everyday life. For example, people often use their hands to communicate when conversations are not possible. We also use gestures when interacting with computers. For example, pinching fingers together then moving them apart is a gesture used for zooming in. This is an example of a multi-touch gesture since it involves more than one finger.

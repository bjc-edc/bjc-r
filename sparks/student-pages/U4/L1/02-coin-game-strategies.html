Coin Game Strategies

In this activity, you'll refine your strategy for the coin game.

  1. What strategies did you try with the coin game?
  2. Describe your favorite strategy precisely.
  3. Compare your strategy with someone else's. How are they similar or different?
  4. Play two strategy against each other to see which works best (and when).
  5. Which strategies work best? Are they more or less the same strategy or are they fundamentally different?

Playing the Coin Game

In this activity, you'll learn to plan a game that you'll soon teach an artificial intelligence (AI) to play.
  1. Set up the game: You need 10 coins (or rocks, figurines, etc.)
  2. Understand the rules:
    • This is a two-player game.
    • On your turn, you can take away either 1 or 2 coins from the table.
    • The person who takes last coin wins!
  3. Play the game a few times with a partner and try to develop a strategy for winning.
    1. What are the "losing numbers"? For example, if you leave 1 coin on the table, you know you'll lose.
    2. What are the "winning numbers"? For example, if you leave 3 coins on the table, then no mater what the other person does, you can win.
    3. If you play first, can you win for sure? If so, how? What if you play second?
  5. Summarize your strategy.
  6. Discuss your strategies and adapt your own based on what you learn.

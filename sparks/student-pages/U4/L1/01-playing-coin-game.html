Playing the Coin Game

In this activity, you'll learn to plan a game that you'll later train a program to play.

  1. Gather 10 coins (or counters, stones, etc.) and a partner.
  2. Learn the rules:
    • Take turns. On your turn, you can take either 1 or 2 coins.
    • Whoever takes the last coin wins!
  3. Play the game a lot of times and with different people; try to develop a strategy for winning as often as possible.

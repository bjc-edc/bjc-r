3D Drawing with Beetle Blocks

In this lab, you will work with a 3D version of Snap! called "Beetle Blocks."

  1. Open Beetle Blocks.
  2. Look through each of the palletes. Look for new blocks that aren't in Snap! and experiment with some of them.
  3. Locate the go to x y z block in the blue "Motion" palette and the text block in the teal "Shapes" palette. Use them together to write a program to produce a 3D message.
    Make an image of something like "BJC NYC" with fancy 3Dness (diagonals , one in front of other, etc.

Rules of the Internet

What rules should there be for what you can or cannot do on the Internet?

  1. With a partner, think of ways that people can be harmed by what is put on the Internet.
  2. Think about the following questions. First, think about how you would answer these questions. Then come up with some reasons for why these questions are complex to answer.
    • Should people be allowed to say and post anything they want on the Internet?
    • If someone is harmed by something on the Internet, who should be held responsible?
    • Should certain vulnerable populations (such as young children) be protected on the Internet? How should they be protected?

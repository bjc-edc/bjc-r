Assessment Ideas

Project-Based Assessment Option: Survey Project

Students learned how export blocks from one Snap! project and import them into another on Unit 2 Lab 4 Page 2: Making a Mathematical Library.

Based on their work with lists, have students create a survey program that answers a question of their choosing and meets these specifications. Their project should collect data from the user, store the data in a list, and process the data to answer their research question by using one or more of the list-processing blocks they learned and/or built in Unit 2.

Before class, decide on a minimum number of responses for students to collect (based on your class size and group dynamics). During class, ensure that students create a research question before designing their survey and then use their results to answer the question when they are finished coding.

Students with ideas for other projects that use one or more of these list-processing blocks are encouraged to discuss these interests as an alternative project-based assessment.

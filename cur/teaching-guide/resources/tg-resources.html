  • AP CS Principles Resources - collection of links and documents from College Board

Greater BJC Community Resources

  • bjc.berkeley.edu - the BJC site at University of California, Berkeley
  • Classroom Resources generated by BJC master teachers - requires access to BJC Teachers Piazza group (email contact@bjc.berkeley.edu for access)
  • BJC on edX:
    • edX BJC Videos - videos from the all-ages non-AP Massive Open Online Course (MOOC) version of BJC that might be helpful in any BJC context
    • The global, MOOC version of BJC can be found at bjc.link/bjcedx; this is for users who want to use the edX format of BJC instead of bjc.edc.org
    • The customizable, Small Private Online Course (SPOC) version of BJC is accessible by emailing contact@bjc.berkeley.edu; this is for teachers who want to adapt the MOOC version for their own course instead of using bjc.edc.org

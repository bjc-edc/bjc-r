How to Get a Project on Your Mobile

Apple iOS Device:

  1. Save and share the project. If desired, make a tinyurl for it.
  2. On your mobile device, open the project URL in Safari.
  3. At the bottom of the Safari screen, tap the share icon, then select "Add to Home Screen" from the menu that appears.

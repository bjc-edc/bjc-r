Discussion/Writing Prompts 

These prompts can be used in sets or spread across several days and are for either individual or collective reflection.

  • Pair Programming (Lab 1)
    • What is pair programming? How does it work?
    • What are the benefits of pair programming?
    • What are the behaviors of an effective pair programming team?
  • Loops (Lab 2)
    • What is a loop?
    • What are some examples of looping commands?
    • What are some benefits of using loops?
    • What happens if you put a loop inside a loop? (Prepare some examples for students to decode.)
    • What are some examples of projects or tasks that would benefit from coding with loops?

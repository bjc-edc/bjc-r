Week 1 Assignments

Optional assignments for class discussion, silent work, or homework (for use anytime during or after the indicated lab):

  • BJC Hour of Code (Consider assigning even before school starts.)
  • How do you interact with computers and digital media? This initial writing assignment can help you gauge both students' writing abilities and familiarity with computers. This is also their first assignment on Google docs, so you can go over the mechanics of accessing their assignment. This assignment can also be completed offline for those without the necessary access at home. (Lab 1)
  • Consider assigning some of the Week 1 discussion prompts as writing prompts.
    • Not sure what this is: "Paper and pencil programming problems link" Anyone? -MF
  • Kaleidoscope Investigation (Lab 2)
  • Mega-Crayons, an adaptation to the Kaleidoscope investigation, in which students draw with multiple, multi-colored sprites at once according to the location of the mouse. In a Mega Crayons program, the mimicking sprites are only translated (offset), not reflected, so the mathematics might be more accessible to some students. (Lab 2)
  • You may wish to specifically assign one or more of the optional "Take it Further" problems:
    • Creating a drawing script (Lab 1, Exploring snap Code)
    • Making a script to draw an equilateral triangle (Lab 1, Getting to Know the snap Interface)
    • Making other interesting designs (Lab 1, Getting to Know the snap Interface)
    • Investigating polygons with more sides (Lab 2, The Repeat Block)
    • Determining the total degrees in drawing a regular 9-pointed star (Lab 2, The Repeat Block)
    • Drawing a regular 5-pointed star (Lab 2, The Repeat Block)
    • Determining the total degrees in drawing the regular 5-pointed star (Lab 2, The Repeat Block)
    • Orienting the star (Lab 2, The Repeat Block)
    • Counting backwards from 100 by tens (Lab 2, The For Block)
    • Nesting squares (Lab 2, The For Block)
    • Nesting polygons (Lab 2, The For Block)
    • Counting by hundredths (Lab 2, The For Block)

Bug Dance Animation

Needs work still... --MF

Students load a Snap! project that has costumes and custom blocks, and create their own custom blocks that switch costumes to make a character dance.

Students will:

  • Practice pair programming
  • Writing scripts to change sprite costumes

Look for:

  • Be on the lookout for especially creative projects.

Tips

  • Students may enjoy creating their own costumes for the "dance" (either taking pictures of themselves in different positions or drawing their own sketches of a character.)

Correlation with AP CS Principles Framework:

Computational Thinking Practices:

  • P6 Collaborating Collaborate with another student in solving a computational problem.
  • P6 Collaborating Exchange knowledge and feedback with a partner or team member.

Essential Knowledge:

  • EK 5.1.3B Collaboration facilitates multiple perspectives in developing ideas for solving problems by programming.
  • EK 5.1.3F Effective communication between participants is required for successful collaboration when developing programs.

Hints

  • Find a method for students to submit snap projects that makes sense to you. Here's one suggestion: In snap, students can choose "Export project..." from the file menu. This brings up text in XML. Students can right-click on the page and "Save As..." to a local folder. Give students directions on naming their files (e.g. Kaleidoscope-first-last.xml), then have them send this file to you. When you go to display their work, you can open snap, then choose "Import..." and the file.

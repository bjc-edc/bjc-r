Programming Lab 1: Conditional Blocks

In this lab, students focus attention on using conditionals—especially if-else, if, but also repeat-until—to control the behavior of their programs based on whatever conditions the students test for (using predicates like < or =). Small projects focus on using multiple if statements to distinguish multiple conditions (e.g., recognizing "red," "yellow," and "blue" in the traffic signal task, and distinguishing various spellings of nouns in order to spell the plurals of those nouns. Distinguishing conditions and basing action on the distinction will remain central to all future programming.

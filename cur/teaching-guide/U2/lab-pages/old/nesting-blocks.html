    • Mathematics Note: Angles in polygons. The script that draws a triangle:

      has three turns of 120°. To see why, imagine walking around an equilateral triangle, keeping track of how much you turn in total. When you get back to where you started, you'll have turned 360°. But you made 3 equal turns, so each turn is \frac{1}{3} of 360° or 120°.

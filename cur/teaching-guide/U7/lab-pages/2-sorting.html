Programming Lab 2: Sorting

Pulled from old version --MF

Students have already seen a quadratic-time sort algorithm in Unit 4;[Citation needed.] here we use branched recursion to introduce a faster algorithm, mergesort, which involves two recursive calls to sort half-size lists. A "Take It Further" at the end hints at the idea of O(n log n) algorithms.

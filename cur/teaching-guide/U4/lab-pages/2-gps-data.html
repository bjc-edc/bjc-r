Programming Lab 2: GPS Data

In this lab, students use Snap! to model and process a batch of GPS coordinates representing the locations of potential clients for a new restaurant in New York City. Beginning with the list of lists of GPS coordinates that they built in Lab 1, students create an appropriately scaled model of the data and analyze it to determine the optimal location for the new business.

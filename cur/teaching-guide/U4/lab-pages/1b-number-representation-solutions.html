  • Page 2: Binary Sequences.
    • Take It Further (Extension Activities)
      1. What's the rightmost digit in the binary representation of 15551212? 0 because 15551212 is even.
      2. What's the rightmost bit ("binary digit") of 123456789? 1 because 123456789 is odd.
      3. Develop a rule for finding the rightmost bit of any base 10 number. If the rightmost digit of the base 10 number is even the rightmost bit of its binary representation will be 0; if it is odd, it will be 1.

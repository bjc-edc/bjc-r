On this page, you will learn about online harassment and abuse.

  1. What are some different forms cyberbullying can take?
    • Conduct some online research. Use these resources, or find your own:
      • How to defeat Internet bullies (CS Monitor)
      • Cyber Bullying (Violence Prevention Works)
      • What methods work with the different kinds of cyberbullies? (stopcyberbullying.org)
      • Six Unforgettable CyberBullying Cases (NoBullying.com)
  2. Have a class discussion. First, share some of your findings from the online research about the different forms cyberbullying can take. Then, together, discuss the following:
    • What are some ways to effectively address this issue?
    • What are challenges to addressing cyberbullying?

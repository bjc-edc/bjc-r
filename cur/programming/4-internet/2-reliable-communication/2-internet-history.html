A Brief History of the Internet

The Internet began in the 1960s with the connection of the first 4 computers the Arpanet, the first packet-switched network with TCP/IP protocols. Email was invented around the same time. However, the World Wide Web wasn't invented until over 20 years later, in 1989. Then in 1993, Search engines were created as it became unrealistic to have one a complete list of all servers. The term "Web 2.0" was coined in 1999 to describe a gradual shift in content generated by large corporations to content from individual users (blogs, photos, vlogs, etc.) via social networking.

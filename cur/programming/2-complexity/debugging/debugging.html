An Introduction to Debugging

On this page, you will learn about some good programming habits and the basics of debugging in Snap!.

Good Programming Habits

  • Assemble neat and organized code.
  • Document your program using comments in Snap!.
    Click for a demo.
  • Use sensible naming of variables. Take advantage of Snap!'s flexibility with variable names including use of spaces.
  • Use of abstraction to hide complexity of code.

What is Debugging?

Debugging is the art of identifying errors in computer programs and finding ways of resolving them.

Debugging Principles

  • Recognize the existence of a bug
  • Reproduce several times the error that the bug creates
  • Find the source of the error
  • Identify the specific cause of the error
  • Fix the error
  • Test thoroughly including consideration of special cases

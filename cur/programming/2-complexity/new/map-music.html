  3. To invert a melody means to make a new melody in which each increase in pitch becomes a decrease, and vice versa. So, the result of inverting the notes {60, 64, 67, 72, 60} would be {60, 56, 53, 48, 60}. (From 60 to 64 is a move up by 4, so in the result we move down by 4 to get 56, and so on.)

Improving Gossip by Making a New Block

Making a New Block

gossip2 always starts with "Oh, but...." That can get boring.

In this activity, you will create a block that randomly chooses starter phrases like "Oh, but" or "And did you hear that" or "Who cares!" (or whatever you like).

  1. Open your project, U1L1-Gossip. (Make sure you are logged in.)

  2. Now edit gossip2 and slide your new block in to replace the fixed text "Oh, but...."

Checking and debugging

It's good practice to save your work often. You will also want to give your programs a good workout, testing them to see if you are satisfied with the way they work. In general, first test the parts that you've just changed, and if they work well, then test the whole program.
  1. Check it out! Click gossip2 a few times to make sure it's working the way you want. Fix any things that still need work.
  2. Now switch back to the gray sprite and click on its script to check the whole program.

There are three articles in English: "the", "a", and "an."

There are several posessive pronouns in English; the ones "my," "your," "her," "his," "its," "our," and "their" are used as determiners, comparable to articles: "the book" and "my book" are similar. (By contrast, "mine," "yours," etc. are used in different contexts: "the book is mine," not "mine book.")

Recursive Tree, Part 1

Our goal is to draw a fractal tree. Notice that the picture contains smaller versions of itself...

A fractal is a repeating pattern made up of copies (or slight variations) of the same shape.

You will build up your fractal tree by using scripts within scripts to create the branches that are smaller copies of the same tree.

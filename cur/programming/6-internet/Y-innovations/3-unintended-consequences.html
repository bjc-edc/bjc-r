Unintended Consequences

  1. What are some beneficial effects of your innovation? List a few.
  2. What are some harmful effects of your innovation? List a few
  3. Get together with a partner and tell each other briefly about your innovations. Then, together, brainstorm more potential harmful effects of your innovation.
    • How might people misuse your innovation?
    • What would happen if something happened to the data used by your innovation?
    • Can people use your innovation to harm others?
    • Could the wrong people take advantage of your innovation somehow?
    • Could someone "cheat" using your innovation?
  4. Write a paragraph description (100 words) of the beneficial and harmful effects of your innovation.
  1. For the AP Explore Task, you must also make a visual artifact to illustrate a potential beneficial or harmful effect of your innovation.
    • Make a visualization, graphic, or movie that provides additional insight to explain, clarify, or depict the beneficial or harmful effect.
    • Write a summary (50 words) to describe how your visual artifact illustrates the benefit or harm.
  2. This lab was mostly looking at someone else's innovation. Now think of how you would address this issue. Design your own innovation to address this issue.

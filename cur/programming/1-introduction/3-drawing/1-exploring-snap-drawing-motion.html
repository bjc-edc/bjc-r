Drawing and Motion

What you will do: Create blocks that let you draw circles, stars, and polygons and explore art and graphic design.

What you will learn: loops to repeat processes, using Snap! to automate calculations, giving blocks inputs to let them do a wider variety of jobs, reporting and setting locations on the stage and controlling sprite movement and direction, and using and controlling color and other pen attributes.

Possible projects this lab will prepare you for: You might draw national flags, model the work of artists like Josef Albers,Vassily Kandinsky, Piet Mondrian,Theo van Doesburg, or Kazimir Malevich, or create new art in your own style.

In general, teachers should find appropriate news articles to share with the class. Choose a mix of news stories, some which show the promise of technology and others which expose negative aspects. Some news sources and example news stories are given below.

News Sources

  • ACM TechNews Archives (updated 3 times a week)
  • Communications of the ACM RSS feeds
  • Computer Weekly RSS feeds
  • Google news search for "computer"

What are some computing innovations that have a positive impact on society? A negative impact?

  • As a class, brainstorm some of these computing innovations.
    • As you use the Internet for research, make sure you keep track of and cite your sources.
  • Write about several computing innovations. Identify the innovation, describe briefly how the innovation works (what it does), what problem the innovation was meant to address, and any potential problems the innovation introduces.
  • In a group of 2-3 people, choose one computing innovation and produce a short video that explains the innovation and some of its impacts.

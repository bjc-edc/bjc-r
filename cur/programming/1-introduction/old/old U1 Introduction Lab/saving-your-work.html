Saving Your Work

From time to time, save your work.

  1. You must first have an account. If you do not already have one, make one now. At the top left corner of the Snap! browser window, find the cloud-shaped button in the toolbar:

  2. Click it, select the "sign up" option in the menu, and follow the instructions. After creating your account, check your email to get your initial password.

    You may change your password at any time, using the cloud-button . Always choose passwords that you will easily remember but that are hard for others to figure out.

  3. That didn't save your work. It just created a space for your work. Now, use the File button in the tool bar to save your work so far. Give it a name that will help you remember what the project is.

    Any time you want that work back, you will use the same button and select "Open..." to see a list of your files.

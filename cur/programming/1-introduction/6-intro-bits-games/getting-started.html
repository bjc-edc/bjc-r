Getting Started

What are some of the ways computers affect your life?

  1. Think about your relationship with technology. Write a short "technology autobiography" describing your relationship. Consider answering some or all of the following:
    • How long have you been using technology?
    • How do you currently use technology?
    • What are three adjectives you would use to describe your relationship?
    • What about technology would you like to explore further?

What counts as technology, anyway?

  • Is a pencil technology?
  • Is food technology?

    • Is a frozen dinner technology?

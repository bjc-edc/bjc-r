Gossip and Simple Sentences

What you will do: In this lab, you will program your computer so that two sprites gossip with each other, picking randomly from words and ideas that you specify.
Keep it friendly!

What you will learn: You will learn to create your own blocks that you can use just like the blocks that Snap! provides. You will learn how to create lists of data, select data from those lists, and combine data in new ways. In this project, the data will be words and phrases that you supply.

On your own: You can, if you like, give the sprites costumes you choose, or make them gossip only when you click on them. Later, you will use gossip-like programs in other projects.

Teacher note: edit the project, insert your name, three or four students' names, and some famous name the students all know; keep "my cat," and something else silly

Editing blocks in a working program

You can improve this program by editing who and does what to give them more (or different) language.

    TO EDIT ANY  BLOCK,
    • Control-click (or right-click) on that block and choose "edit..."

    • Click OK when you're satisfied. (Or click "Cancel" if you don't want to make changes).

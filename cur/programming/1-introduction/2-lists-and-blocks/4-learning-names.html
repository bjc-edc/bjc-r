Learning a New Name

Project: Meet and Greet

Imagine a greeter at the door of a restaurant. The greeter asks each new guest, "what's your name?" If Mario comes in and the greeter recognizes the name, she says "Nice to see you again, Mario." If the greeter doesn't recognize the name, she says, "Nice to meet you, Mario," and remembers Mario's for next time. The goal of this project is to build a script that acts like the greeter.

Getting Started

There are two parts that will make this work:

  1. Create a list to store the names of people the greeter knows.
  2. Create a block that gives the right greeting to each customer.

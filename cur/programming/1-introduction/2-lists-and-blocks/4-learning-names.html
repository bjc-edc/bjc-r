Learning a New Name

Project: Meet and Greet

Imagine a greeter at the door of a restaurant. The greeter asks each new guest, "what's your name?" If Mario comes in and the greeter recognizes the name, she says "Nice to see you again, Mario." If the greeter doesn't recognize the name, she says, "Nice to meet you, Mario," and remembers Mario's name for next time. The goal of this project is to build a script that acts like the greeter.

Getting Started

There are two parts that will make this work:

  1. Create a list to store the names of people the greeter knows.
  2. Create a script that gives the right greeting to each customer. First, the greeter asks the guest for his or her name. Then the greeter has to make a decision: is the guest in the list of people I know or not?
    1. if people I know contains the guest's name, say "nice to see you again, guest name."
    2. esle say "nice meet you, guest name," and add the guest's name to the list of known people.

    3. The decision making block is made with if-else

Improving Gossip by Making a New Block

What you will do: gossip2 always starts with "Oh, but...." That can get boring. That starter-phrase could change, just the way the people and action change.

On this page, you will improve gossip2. You will learn to build and name a completely new block that randomly chooses phrases like "Oh, but" or "Did you hear?" or "Who cares!" (or...), and you will learn how to use your new block to replace a part in an already existing block.

Checking and debugging

Whenever you program, give your programs a workout, testing to see if they do what you want.
Generally, you should first test parts that you've just built or changed.
Then, if the parts work, test the whole program.

  1. Check it out! Click gossip2 a few times to see if you like how it works. Fix whatever still needs work.

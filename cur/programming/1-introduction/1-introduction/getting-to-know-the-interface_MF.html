Getting to Know the snap Interface

Naming the sections of the snap window makes it easy to refer to them.

Tool Bar

The Toolbar at the top has buttons for:

  • the File menu (for saving and loading your projects),
  • the Cloud menu (for accessing your snap account),
  • the Settings menu (for advanced options),
  • stage resizing buttons ( and ),
  • and project control buttons (green flag , pause , and stop ).
  • The snap logo offers more information including the the snap manual.

Palette

The palette contains the blocks that you use to tell the computer what to do. The blocks are organized into eight categories according to the kind of thing they do: Motion, Looks, Sound, Pen, Control, Sensing, Operators, and Variables.

The Stage is where sprites—little on-screen robots—act out the code you write. You can change the background design of your stage depending on what you want your program to do.

Position on the Stage

The stage starts out blank white. You can change its background to show the (x, y) coordinates, if you like. in the usual way where x represents the horizontal position, from -240 (left) to 240 (right), and y represents the vertical position, from -180 (bottom) to 180 (top). Here's a picture:
To make the snap stage look like this:
  1. In the Sprite Corral, select the Stage.
  2. Then, choose "Backgrounds" from the File menu .
  3. And choose "xy-grid.gif".
Just like in algebra, the center of the stage is called the origin and has coordinates (0, 0). The black sprite above is to the right of the origin, so its x position is positive. Since it is also below the origin, its y position is negative. Here, each grid line represents 20 steps.
Unlike in algebra, the numbers naming each direction start with 0 at the top. The black sprite above is pointing to the right, so its direction is 90.

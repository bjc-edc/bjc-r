The forever block is the first block you have seen that holds, or wraps around, other blocks. We call this a C block because of its shape. As the name "forever" implies, it will run the blocks inside it again and again and again and ... well, forever.
You can stop all scripts by clicking on the stop sign at the top right corner of the snap window. This is equivalent to executing the in the "Control" palette.

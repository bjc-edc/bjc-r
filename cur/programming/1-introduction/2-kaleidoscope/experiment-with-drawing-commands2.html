Experiment with Drawing Commands

Assemble a collection of blocks from the "Motion" and "Pen" tabs into a script that draws a square or triangle or some other a simple picture. You will almost certainly want at least these blocks:        and .
Reminders:
  • Once the pen is down, it stays down even in a different script. If you want your script to move the sprite without leaving a trace, use the block to "lift the pen."
  • If you want, you can use watchers to show the direction of the sprite or its x and y coordinates.

Making the Game More Challenging

At this point, the game doesn't encourage the player to play quickly. But if the goal is to make Alonzo disappear by clicking him and you make him reappear whenever he moves on his own, you can make your game more challenging to play.

  1. Figure out where to add a change (ghost) effect by (-5) block to your script to make Alonzo get more opaque (less see-through) any time he moves without being clicked.
  2. Play your game a few times, and adjust your code to get a level of difficulty that you like.
    3. You can change the ghost effect input number and the wait time to make the game easier or harder.

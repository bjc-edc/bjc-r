Project: Meet and Greet

Imagine a greeter at the door of a restaurant. The greeter asks each new guest, "what's your name?" If Mario comes in and the greeter recognizes the name, she says "Nice to see you again, Mario." If she doesn't recognize the name, she says, "Nice to meet you, Mario," and she remembers Mario's for next time. The goal of this project is to build a script that acts like the greeter

Getting Started


Creating a Snap! account lets you save your programs to the cloud.
  1. In the large frame below, click the cloud menu in the tool-bar.

    Select the "sign up" option in the menu, and follow the instructions.
    Then, check your email to get your initial password.
    You may change your password at any time, using the cloud menu.
  1. Log in using your user name and the password you received.
  2. Select "Change Password..." from the cloud menu.
  3. Change your password to something easy for you to remember but hard for others to guess.

Logging in to Your Snap! Account


You should be logged in whenever you are coding.
Here's how to check to see if you are logged in.
  1. Click the Cloud menu in the tool-bar:
    • IF it says "logout" and then your user name, you are already logged in.
    • IF it says someone else's user name, log out and then log in with your own user name.
    • ELSE, choose "login..." and enter your user name and password.

Continue to Code or Log Out


  1. Do you have time to continue programming right now?
    • IF you do, click NEXT (top of this instruction page) to see what to do next.
    • IF you are done for now, log out (using the Cloud menu).
      Remember to log out any time you are done programming.

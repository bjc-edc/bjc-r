There's always something more you can do to make a program better. On this page, you'll improve your game by making Alonzo welcome the player when the green flag () is clicked.

Clicking the green flag () starts all scripts that begin with the when green flag is clicked hat block. You can stop all running scripts by clicking the red stop sign () at the top right of the Snap! window.

  1. If it isn't open already, open your U1L1-ClickAlonzo project.
  2. Create a second script that makes Alonzo a welcome message whenever the green flag is clicked. (Alonzo might say, "Welcome to 'Click Alonzo!' Keep clicking me if you can!")
    You can do this using just 3 blocks including the block at the top of the script.
  3. Make that message disappear when the player clicks on Alonzo.
    The say block with an empty input will make the sprite say nothing.
  4. We should double-check that this is everything we want the app to do at this point. --MF, 5/17/19

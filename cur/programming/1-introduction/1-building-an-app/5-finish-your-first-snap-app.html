Improving Your Game

There's always something more you can do to make a program better. On this page, you'll improve your game by making Alonzo welcome the player when the green flag () is clicked.

Clicking the green flag button in the top right corner of the window () starts all scripts that begin with the when green flag is clicked hat block.

  1. If it isn't open already, open your U1L1-ClickAlonzo project.
  2. Create a second script that makes Alonzo say () a welcome message whenever the green flag is clicked. (Alonzo might say, "Welcome to 'Click Alonzo!' Keep clicking me if you can!")
    You can do this using just 3 blocks including the block at the top of the script.
  3. Make that message disappear when the player clicks on Alonzo.
    The say block with an empty input will make the sprite say nothing.
  4. Check that your app does everything you want:
    • When the green flag is clicked, Alonzo should say his message.
    • When Alonzo is clicked, the message should disappear, and Alonzo should move to a random spot on the stage and face the other way.
    • And from your work on previous pages, every time Alonzo is clicked, he should face the other way and get a little lighter, but he should start to reappear any time he moves without being clicked.

Sharing Your App

On this page, you will learn how to get a link for sharing your app and how to load your app on a mobile device.
  1. Make sure your project is saved.
  2. Choose "Open..." from the File menu, select the file from the project list by clicking on its name (in this lab, "U1L1-ClickAlonzo"), and click "Share." (The video below shows this process.)
  3. Click "Yes" you want to publish. (Then click either "Cancel" or "Open" to close that menu. It doesn't matter which.)
  4. Copy the URL (web address) from the top of your browser window. You can use that link to share the app.
    5. You can also make a tincture for your game to make typing the URL easier.

      1. On your mobile device, open the project URL from step 3 above in Safari.
      2. At the bottom of the Safari screen, tap the share icon, then select "Add to Home Screen" from the menu that appears.

Sharing Your App and Loading It on a Mobile Device

Now, load the app on your mobile device.

  1. In Snap! on your computer, save your project. Then, choose "Save as..." from the File menu, select the file (in this lab, "U1L1-HourofCode"), and click "Share".
  2. Then, say "Yes" you want to publish, and copy the URL (web address) from the top of your browser window. You can use this link to share the app.
    You can also make a tinyurl for your game to make typing the URL easier.
  3. On an Apple iOS device:
    1. On your mobile device, open the project URL in Safari.
    2. At the bottom of the Safari screen, tap the share icon, then select "Add to Home Screen" from the menu that appears.
  4. On an Android device:
    1. On your mobile device, open the project URL in Chrome.

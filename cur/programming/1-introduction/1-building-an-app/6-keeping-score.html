Make It a Game

Now the fun part: You'll make your app into a real game. When clicked, Alonzo will keep moving around the screen. You try to click on Alonzo, and if you succeed, Alonzo turns a little lighter in color and jumps to another spot. If you miss, Alonzo just jumps and doesn't change color. The goal of the game is to make Alonzo turn so light that it can't be seen.

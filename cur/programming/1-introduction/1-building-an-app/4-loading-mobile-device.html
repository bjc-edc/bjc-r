Sharing Your Game

On this page, you will share your app, and you can load it on a mobile device.
  1. Get a web address to share your app...
    1. Make sure your project is saved.
    2. Choose "Open..." from the File menu.
    3. Select the file from the project list (in this lab, "U1L1-ClickAlonzo").
    4. Click "Share" then click "Yes" to publish. Then click either "Cancel" or "Open" to close that menu. (It doesn't matter which.)

      5. To locate the file you are searching for, you can type "U1" in the search bar to see only a list of your Unit 1 files.

    5. When you share the project, the URL (web address) at the top of the browser window changes. Copy the entire new URL from the top of your browser window, and use that link to share the app.

      6. If you change something in the project after sharing it, the changes are reflected in what other people see. You don't have to re-share it.

  2. Log out and let your partner log in. Open the link you created with the first partner's account, and save the project in the second partner's account so that you both have a copy.
    The partner has a copy—not a shared file. So, if you change the project in one person's account, it will not change in the other person's account; you will need to save it to their account again.

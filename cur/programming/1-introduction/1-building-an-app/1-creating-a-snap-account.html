Getting Started with Snap!

In this lab, you'll program an app that you can share with others and play on your phone.

On this page, you'll learn how to create your own account to store your code, how to log in and out, and how to change your account password.

Creating an Account

  1. If you haven't already opened snap, open it now: http://snap.berkeley.edu/run
  2. In the Snap! window that opens, click the Cloud menu (), select "Signup...", and follow the instructions.
  3. Then, check your email to get your initial password, and log in.
    4. You may change your password at any time using the cloud menu ().
  4. Now, select "Change Password..." from the Cloud menu, and change your password to something easy for you to remember but hard for others to guess.

Logging in to and out of Your Snap! Account

You should be logged in whenever you are coding. Here's how to check if you are logged in:

  1. Click the Cloud menu in the tool-bar.

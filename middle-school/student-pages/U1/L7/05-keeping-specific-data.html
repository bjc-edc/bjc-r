Snap! has two built-in blocks that may be useful to your data analysis:

  • The block takes a list as input and reports the length of that list.
  • The block filters a list, reporting back only the items that met certain criteria.

  1. If your project isn't open already, log in to Snap!, and open your "Data Dos" project.
  2. Test the length block by using it to count the number of responses to your survey.
